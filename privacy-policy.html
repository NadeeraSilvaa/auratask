1. Introduction

Welcome to AuraTasks! DevUno ("we", "our", "us") operates the AuraTasks mobile application ("the Service", "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using AuraTasks, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not download, install, or use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you provide directly when using AuraTasks:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name (optional), and profile picture (optional)
  • Task Data: Tasks, subtasks, notes, descriptions, categories, priorities, due dates, and reminders you create
  • Recurring Tasks: Recurrence patterns, intervals, and schedules
  • Location Data: Geographic coordinates and addresses you specify for location-based reminders (only when you enable this feature)
  • Time Tracking: Time spent on tasks and time estimates
  • Settings: App preferences including theme settings and notification preferences

2.2. Information Collected Automatically

When you use AuraTasks, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, features used, and interaction patterns to improve our service
  • Crash Reports: Technical data about app crashes and errors to help us fix bugs and improve stability
  • Analytics Data: Task completion rates, productivity metrics, and aggregated usage statistics

2.3. Location Information

AuraTasks requests location permissions only when you choose to use location-based reminders. We collect your location data solely for the purpose of sending reminders when you arrive at or leave specified locations. Location data is collected in the background only when location reminders are enabled.

Important: You can disable location services at any time through your device settings or within the App settings. Location data is only used when you explicitly enable location-based reminders.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and Maintain the Service: Create and manage your account, sync your data across devices, and deliver core app functionality
  • Location-Based Reminders: Monitor your location (only when enabled) and send notifications when you arrive at or depart from specified locations
  • Improve Our Service: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, enhance features, and optimize app performance
  • Personalization: Provide personalized task suggestions and recommendations based on your usage patterns
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide technical support
  • Security: Protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1. Data Storage

All your data is securely stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform), which provides enterprise-grade security and reliability:

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure authentication system protecting your account
  • Cloud Firestore: NoSQL database storing your tasks, settings, and app data
  • Firebase Storage: Secure storage for profile pictures and user-generated content

4.2. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing and authentication tokens
  • Access Controls: Your data is only accessible by you and is protected by authentication
  • Regular Security Updates: We keep our infrastructure and dependencies up to date with security patches
Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your information to the best of our ability.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1. Access Your Data

You can access all your data at any time within the App. Additionally, AuraTasks provides data export features:

  • JSON Export: Complete backup of all your data in JSON format
  • CSV Export: Export tasks to spreadsheet-compatible CSV format

7.2. Delete Your Data

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data:

  • In-App Deletion: Delete your account directly from the App settings

You can update or correct your information at any time within the App. This includes your display name, profile picture, tasks, and all other user-generated content.

7.4. Data Portability

You can export your data in machine-readable formats (JSON, CSV) using the export features in the App settings.

7.5. Opt-Out

You can:
